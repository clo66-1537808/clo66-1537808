About Us

We care about makeup.

Se-Four-A cares about our products being as natural and organic as possible for our users. Our team has always wanted to have a makeup line that focuses strictly on organic, vegan friendly makeup so we decided to come up with our own line. We encourage you to come and try on our products and try any free samples. Take it home and fall in love with it.
Our company started in 2014 and ever since we have been improving and adding products. With all of your support, we continue to thrive. We appreciate everyone who joins our efforts in making makeup organic and natural.
Feel free to join our Rewards program and collect points to earn free makeup!
